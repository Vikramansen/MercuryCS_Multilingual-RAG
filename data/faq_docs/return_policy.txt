# Return & Refund Policy

## Return Window
You can return items within 30 days of delivery.
Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached.

## Refund Process
Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for inspection.
Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
It may take an additional 3-5 business days for the funds to appear in your account.

## Exchanges
We currently do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the item for a refund and place a new order.

## Non-Returnable Items
Gift cards, downloadable software, and intimate apparel are final sale and cannot be returned.
