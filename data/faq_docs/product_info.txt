# Product Information

## Wireless Headphones X1
- Battery Life: Up to 30 hours with ANC off, 24 hours with ANC on.
- Charging: USB-C quick charge (10 min charge = 5 hours playback).
- Features: Active Noise Cancellation, Transparency Mode, Multipoint Bluetooth.
- Weight: 250g.

## Smart Watch Pro
- Display: 1.4 inch AMOLED always-on display.
- Battery: Up to 7 days typical use.
- Sensors: Heart rate, SpO2, GPS, Sleep tracking.
- Water Resistance: 5ATM (swim proof).

## Ergonomic Chair
- Material: Breathable mesh back, high-density foam seat.
- Adjustability: Lumbar support, armrest height/angle, seat depth, recline tension.
- Max Weight Capacity: 300 lbs.
- Warranty: 5 years.
