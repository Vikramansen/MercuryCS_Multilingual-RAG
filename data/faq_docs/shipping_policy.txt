# Shipping Policy

## Delivery Times
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days.
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days.
International shipping can take up to 14 business days depending on customs.

## Shipping Costs
Standard shipping is free for orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $5.99 applies.
Express shipping costs $15.99 regardless of order value.

## Tracking
Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.
You can track your order on our website using the 'Track Order' link.
